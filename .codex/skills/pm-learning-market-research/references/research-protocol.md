# Market Research Protocol

## Source Hierarchy

Prefer, in order:

1. Official company career pages and JD text.
2. Major job boards with visible posting dates.
3. Reputable salary or market reports with publication dates.
4. Professional community posts only as qualitative signals.

## Required Citation Fields

- Source name
- Link
- Snapshot date
- Role/location
- Key quoted or paraphrased requirement
- Confidence level: high, medium, low

## Analysis Rules

- Separate high-frequency requirements from interesting one-offs.
- Do not generalize from fewer than 3 relevant samples unless clearly labeled.
- Keep salary conclusions cautious unless sources are recent and comparable.
- Translate requirements into three buckets:
  - Already has evidence.
  - Needs learning/project output.
  - Needs better packaging.

## Output Bias

The result should improve one of:

- Next learning route.
- Portfolio project choice.
- Resume positioning.
- Interview preparation.
- Company/role targeting.
