# Career Packaging Rubric

## Strong AI PM Evidence

Strong packaging shows:

- Role target and audience.
- User/problem context.
- Product decision and tradeoff.
- AI system quality concern: evaluation, trace, cost, latency, fallback, monitoring, risk, or human review.
- Business or delivery value.
- Concrete artifact or observable result.
- Personal contribution.

## Weak Packaging Signals

Avoid:

- "负责需求分析/项目推进" without specific scope.
- Overclaiming architecture ownership without evidence.
- Listing tools without product reasoning.
- Generic AI buzzwords without quality or business implications.
- Numbers that cannot be defended in interview.

## Bullet Formula

Use this pattern:

`[Action] + [object/scope] + [product or business value] + [AI/platform quality mechanism] + [observable result or evidence]`

If metrics are missing, use defensible outputs:

- Demo V1.
- Eval report.
- Case study.
- Interview script.
- PRD or prototype.
- Test pass result.

## Interview Story Formula

1. Why this project mattered.
2. Who used or would use it.
3. What product problem it solved.
4. What AI/product tradeoffs existed.
5. What was built or designed.
6. How quality was evaluated.
7. What the user personally contributed.
8. What would be improved next.
